Economic and Health Matrix

This matrix compares changes in economic and health conditions of a region at the provincial level before new normal (4 June 2020, when Jakarta government announced the discontinuation of PSBB) and after new normal. The economic condition is determined from the calculation of Movement Index. Meanwhile, the health situation is illustrasted by the Covid-19 Intensity Index.

National Covid-19 Index

Covid-19 Intensity Index at the national level, calculated from 4 sub-components of the index: mortality index, peak index, growth index and active cases index. All data were obtained from KawalCOVID19. The higher value of Covid-19 Intensity Index indicates the worsening developments of Covid-19 cases at the provincial level.

National Mobility Index

The movement index at the national level since march 1, 2020 with a moving average over 7 days. A high index value indicates higher mobility on particular days compared to the baseline of March 1, 2020.
